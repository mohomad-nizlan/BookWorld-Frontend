About Us

Welcome to Book World Private Limited, your trusted destination for a vast range of books, from timeless classics to the latest bestsellers. We are passionate about connecting readers with books that inspire, inform, and ignite imagination. Whether you're a student, a casual reader, or an avid book collector, we have something for everyone.

Founded in 2000, Book World started as a small local store, and over the years, we have grown into a beloved online bookstore serving customers across Sri Lanka. We pride ourselves on offering exceptional customer service and a curated selection of books across various genres.
